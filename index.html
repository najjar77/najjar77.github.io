About

In my capacity as a UX designer, I have a solid grounding in the principles of user-centered design and a passion for creating intuitive and captivating digital experiences. I have thorough understanding of user needs and behaviors thanks to my experience using both qualitative and quantitative research methods. This knowledge guides my design choices. I have experience making wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups for both web and mobile applications and am proficient in a number of design tools, including Figma. I enjoy the process of seeing a concept through to implementation, and I take pride in my ability to convert intricate user flows into sophisticated yet simple designs.

UX methods

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have utilized various UX design methods, including

  • User Flows
  • Wireframes & Prototyping
  • User & Usability Testing
  • User Interviews

I've been able to use these techniques in existing projects and have gained experience doing so. I am therefore confident that I can use these techniques to produce user-centered designs.

Work experience

This period allowed me to test my theoretical knowledge in real-world applications. I immersed myself in the development and enhancement of user interfaces, collaborating effectively with an interdisciplinary agile team. This exploratory journey served as a living stage, honing my UX design skills and cultivating a strong sense for intuitive, user-centered interfaces.
